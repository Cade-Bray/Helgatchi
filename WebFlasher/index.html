What to flash
Your browser can't flash over USB. Use desktop Chrome, Edge, or Opera. Serial access was blocked. Reload and allow the port when prompted.

If the flasher offers to erase the device, leave it unchecked — the boxes above control exactly what gets written.

Before you start

  • Use desktop Chrome, Edge, or Opera (Web Serial isn't in Firefox/Safari).
  • Connect the Helgatchi with a data USB-C cable (not charge-only).
  • Pick a version, choose what to flash, click Connect & Install, and select the serial port.
  • Updating firmware keeps your settings and rules. Use Full wipe for a first-time device or a clean reset.
